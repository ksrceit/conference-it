About Us

IC - DISQC 2024 is a premier international conference organized by KSRCE's Department of Information Technology, led by Dr. G. Singaravel and Mr. G. Dharani. It explores diverse aspects of quantum computing, from finance to cryptography, offering a platform for collaboration among researchers, academics, and industry professionals. Accepted abstracts stand a chance for publication in prestigious journals indexed in Web of Science, Scopus, and Google Scholar. Join us to delve into the cutting-edge realm of intermediate scale quantum computing.

What is Our Goal?

Our goal is to advance the field of intermediate scale quantum computing by fostering collaboration, sharing knowledge, and facilitating discussions among researchers, academics, and industry experts. We aim to explore various applications of quantum computing across different domains such as finance, material science, machine learning, natural language processing, cryptography, and more. Through this conference, we strive to promote innovation, drive research forward, and contribute to the development of secure and efficient quantum communication systems. Additionally, we aim to provide a platform for participants to publish their research in esteemed journals or books indexed in Web of Science, Scopus, and Google Scholar, thereby amplifying the impact of their work on the global scientific community.
